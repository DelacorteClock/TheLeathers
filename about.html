MORE ABOUT EMD

What I Do In Addition To Programming

I'm a person with many interests and creative pursuits. I love riding trains and buses just for fun and to travel. Making animated films, paintings and virtual art is my jam. I also enjoy designing virtual sculptures and exploring museums. You'll often find me rocking fashion-forward outfits made of leather and vinyl with my hair painted in exciting colours. Due to my (poor) balancing skills I cruise round on a tricycle. Cooking is a massive hobby and I get a kick out of teaching and running educational clubs. Oh...and my sister and I make cool films featuring toy vehicles. I even know how to operate railway locomotives! Architectural history fascinates me and I'm planning to pursue an architecture degree. Right now my goals are to try my hand at operating a real locomotive, learn fashion manufacturing and eventually master driving a bus.
